Area of Interest

My research area of interest is mainly analytic number theory. At present I am interested in analytic properties of certain generalizations of Euler's constant. Euler's constant has been studied by many mathematicians since the 18th century. For a survey of results related to Euler's constant see here. I am also interested in special values of L-functions and Divisor problems.
