Area of Interest

My research area of interest is mainly analytic number theory. During my PhD I was interested in analytic properties of certain generalizations of the Euler constant. Euler constant has been studied by many mathematicians since the 18th century. For a survey of results related to Euler's constant see here. Currently I am interested in special values of L-functions, L-functions in Selberg Class, application of Euler constant to Divisor problems and Euler-Kronecker constants.
